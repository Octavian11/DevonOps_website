Implementation Spec

Information Architecture

Reduce the main page from 12.3 viewport-heights to ~9.5 by merging redundant sections, collapsing detail-heavy content, and tightening the persuasion sequence. Plus cross-page consistency for /pe/services, /pe/about, and McKinsey blockquote standardization. No content is deleted — it's restructured for faster comprehension.

Item 01
Merge "Choose Your Situation" into "Services & Pricing"
The "Choose Your Situation" section (Evaluating a Target vs. First 100 Days Post-Close) and the "Services & Pricing" section are saying the same thing from different angles. The path cards describe what you get; the pricing cards describe what it costs. They should be one unified decision section.
Critical Merge path cards into pricing section

Move the two path card elements ("Evaluating a Target" and "First 100 Days Post-Close") from their standalone section into the top of the "Services & Pricing" section. They become an introductory sub-section within pricing — not a separate page section.

Design note: The McKinsey blockquotes use padding: 20px 24px (slightly larger than the standard 14px 16px for proof quotes) because they contain longer statistical paragraphs. The border-radius is also 0 8px 8px 0 (slightly larger than the 0 6px 6px 0 on card-internal proof quotes) to feel proportionate to the larger block. This is a deliberate size variation within the shared visual language — same family, slightly larger sibling.
